P-AgBot

Completed (Aug 2025)

I worked in Purdue's Multi-Scale Robotics and Automation Lab with Professor Cappelleri to make a new configuration of P-AgBot, one designed for the autonomous deployment and monitoring of soil moisture sensors developed by the Internet of Things for Precision Agriculture (IoT4Ag) at the University of Pennsylvania. A robust mechanical and electrical system was created and a deep learning-based visual servoing method was developed for motion control. Look out for a paper that will be published at the start of 2026!

#AutonomousRobots #PrecisionAgriculture #SensorDeployment #ComputerVision #DeepLearning

Search and Rescue Robot (SARR)

Completed (Dec 2024)

In MAE 322: Mechanical Design, I was apart of the team Doofenshmirtz Evil Inc. and designed a Search and Rescue Robot for the traversal of rough terrain, including a 8 foot long ramp, dogleg chute, and a 1 foot high wall, and the autonomous delivery of a 1 kilogram med kit. I was the team lead for the med kit delivery system and worked on every aspect of this robot. My team successfully navigated the whole course and delivered the med kit autonomously.

Optimized Airplane Wing

Completed (May 2024)

In MAE 321: Engineering Design, I was a part of the team that designed an optimized airplane wing. Within my team, I helped lead the CAD design in CREO Parametric. Our wing is primarily comprised of a specially designed I-beam and 8 bulkheads. During testing, our 1.33lb beam withstood 245lbs before breaking the test apparatus at 270lbs.

#Aircraft Structures #CREOParametric #Optimization
